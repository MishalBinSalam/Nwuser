?   What is Nearwala app?
-Nearwala is a local Shopping App, which helps you to find products and Services from your    Local Stores and Get up to 50% off.

 ?   Why should I download the nearwala mobile app?
-Pay Less Save More,Finding Offers in your nearest shops, Experiencing Offline shopping along with Online Offers.

 ?   How do i register myself on nearwala?
It is very easy to use, Go to playstore/IOS Download nearwala app, SignUp using your mobile number and Start to search find offers from nearest shop.

 ?   Is nearwala app available in IOS?
-Yes, Nearwala app available in Playstore for android users and in AppStore for iPhone users.

 ?   How do i get notifications on nearwala offers?
-Follow the Shops you want to get notifications.

 ?   How do I know Coupons?
In App just click on MyDeals button below the app, then click on More Coupons It will shows all
Shops list with Coupons in nearest area.

 ?   How do I know Discounts?
Just click on Shops Button below the app, and then click on Happy Hours, you can see list of discount available shops.

 ?   Can i Share products to my friends and Family?
Yes, You can Share Products/Shops to your friends and family by clicking share option which will be shown below every shop.

 ?   Can i Chat with Shop owners directly?
Yes, You can chat with Shop Owners by clicking Chat option given below all shops.
